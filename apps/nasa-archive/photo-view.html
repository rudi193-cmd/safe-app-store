Riggs What do you remember about this one? Start anywhere — a moment, a sound, who was standing next to you when it was taken.
You That's the parking lot at the Orleans. Saturday morning, everyone's lined up getting ready for the ride to Jean. I think that's Buster's Lambretta on the left.
Riggs The Orleans — that's the host hotel, right? And the ride to Jean, that's the dry lake bed run. chk-tunk — Buster's Lambretta, what model was that? And did it make it to Jean or did someone have to stop for it?
You SX 200, I think. It made it but barely. The carb was running rich the whole way. You could smell it three bikes back.
Riggs An SX 200 running rich in the desert — that's a specific kind of misery. The heat would've made it worse, fuel expanding in the float bowl. Three bikes back and you could smell it — that's a story. Did someone look at it when you got to Jean, or did Buster just ride it home like that?
