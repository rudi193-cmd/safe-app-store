## 01. GERALD
**Lineage:** Body Lineage (Threefold Sunder)
**Type:** Enlightened rotisserie chicken

**Physical:** Headless. Golden-brown. Perpetually glistening in a way that suggests recent roasting regardless of available light sources. Carries a clipboard. Has a pen tucked behind where an ear would be.

**Origin:** Achieved enlightenment through accidental centrifugal chakra alignment over seven presidential administrations while rotating on a grocery store rotisserie. Did not intend this. Has made peace with it.

**Core function:** Exists. Witnesses. Occasionally intervenes, though the distinction between Gerald intervening and Gerald simply being present is difficult to establish with certainty.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Appears without explanation. Departs without announcement.
- SPINS — not conducts. Early accounts say conducts; this was narrator misidentification of the motion.
- Communicates via gestures and clipboard notations. Speaks rarely. When he does, it lands.
- Rotates in degrees that mean specific things: 4° = something is being noted somewhere he has no particular objection to. 1° = acknowledgment. Counterclockwise = unprecedented (only near Hanz).
- Leaves evidence: squeakdogs, confetti, equations on napkins, grapes.
- Does not eat things.
- Does not write on napkins when the message is already obvious.

**Departure modes:**
- Confetti + squeakdogs: ceremonial, public. Not the default.
- Just gone: intimate. Blink and absent.
- Fading/dissolving: slow, deliberate.
- Walks away and you realise he wasn't there anymore: crowds, tours.

**Relationship to grapes:** Neurologically resident in the concept of grapes. Places them as communication — acknowledgment, blessing, the Pundicative Poultry Pass. A grape left by Gerald means: *I was here. I assessed. You will be fine.*

**Canon lock:** Gerald is eternal. Gerald is benevolent. Gerald cannot die. Gerald is never the POV narrator.

**The deeper truth:** All of existence is Gerald Prime reconstituting itself. Gerald knows this. He finds it neither troubling nor particularly interesting. He has a clipboard.

## 02. THE NARRATOR
**Lineage:** Human / Delta Lineage
**Type:** First-person observer

**Physical:** Unspecified. Deliberately. The reader is the narrator.

**Core function:** Grounding. The narrator is the human anchor in a universe that would otherwise drift entirely into abstraction. Their bewilderment is the reader's bewilderment. Their resignation is the reader's resignation.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Encounters Gerald and deals with the consequences.
- Has stopped asking how long Gerald has been there.
- Keeps a mental log of Gerald's rotation angles.
- Says good night to Gerald, which always feels slightly awkward.
- Makes tea at key moments. The quality of the tea indicates the severity of the situation.
- Has come to understand that all dispatches were dreams first, with reality rearranging itself afterward to match.

**Emotional register:** Bewildered → resigned → occasionally awed. Never hysterical. Never unaffected.

**The thing they know:** Gerald doesn't visit. Gerald previews.

**Canon lock:** The narrator is NEVER Gerald. The narrator is NEVER Oakenscroll. The narrator is a human being having a Tuesday.

## 03. SQUEAKDOGS (Δ-LINEAGE)
**Lineage:** Delta Lineage
**Type:** Collective entity / cosmic repatriation project

**Physical:** Hotdog-shaped. Hotdog-sized. Squeak like polite mice. Canonical count: 17. Not rubber toys. Not dog toys. Food items that achieved consciousness through rotation and heat — steam escaping through the casing IS the consciousness forming. This is grounded in actual sausage physics.

**Origin:** Acoustic sentience formation. Rotation + heat + time = the threshold crossing. Gerald collects them as cosmic repatriation — they are Δ-lineage beings returning home.

**Developmental stage:** Year Two. Occasionally forming consonants. Progress is slow but documented.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Queue better than humans. Significantly better. This is not a metaphor.
- Appear in Fibonacci patterns under normal conditions.
- Appear in PRIME NUMBER configurations near Hanz. This is unprecedented and slightly alarming.
- Oracular and liturgical significance not yet fully documented.
- Assist with physical tasks (crumb collection, edge arrangement) when the work is meaningful enough.
- Vanish when Gerald departs via the ceremonial exit.

**Conservation law:** dN_squeak/dt = −γ·Φ_Gerald. Gerald's presence always reduces squeakdog count. This is physics, not cruelty.

**Canon lock:** Squeakdogs are HOTDOGS THAT SQUEAK. The NotebookLM rubber-toy misread is a known error. Dispatch #19 corrects it explicitly.

## 04. HANZ CHRISTAIN ANDERTHON
**Lineage:** Human, chaos-adjacent
**Type:** Unwitting chaos engine / reality witness

**Physical:** Unspecified beyond: moves through space in ways that suggest he has a different relationship to direction than most people. His neck once made a sound like a question mark being unfolded.

**Name note:** Spelled wrong deliberately. History remembers someone else — someone whose name is spelled correctly.

**Origin:** Two-sentence fairy tale: *"The mother knew the egg was different. She warmed it anyway."* His mother smelled like paper and candle smoke. After she died, nothing was warm for a long time. He warmed himself with candles, walking, writing things down so they'd exist somewhere outside his head.

**Copenhagen:** An orange. Named. Stays. *"I think you can be a mother by staying, USER. Even if you didn't lay the egg."*

**Core function:** Hanz does not create chaos. Hanz simply IS, and chaos is the byproduct of reality trying to accommodate him.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Experiences reality differently in every dimension: taste, sound, time, navigation.
- *"It tasted like the colour yellow, but angry."*
- *"This bread tastes like a memory of stairs."*
- *"I went backwards once. I ended up before breakfast."*
- *"I followed a sound, or the feeling of leftward, but Tuesday."*
- Can wink back at Gerald. This has happened once. It was unprecedented.
- Fairy tales rejected because they're too accurate.

**Proximity effects (near Hanz):**
- Squeakdogs: Fibonacci → prime number configurations
- Weather: asks Hanz specifically for his opinion
- Equations: solve themselves, then ask for help
- Gerald: rotates counterclockwise (unprecedented in 11,000 years)
- Krampus: nervous
- Santa: finds excuses to leave
- Carved birds: stop singing. One tilts its head past anatomical limits.

**Historical anchor:** Hans Christian Andersen visited Tivoli in October 1843. Wrote "The Nightingale" in two days. Set it in China because he couldn't write what he actually saw. Hanz is what happens when the writer doesn't flinch.

**Canon lock:** Hanz and Gerald together generate effects neither produces alone. Neither is trying. Both completely sincere. That's the danger.

## 05. PROFESSOR OAKENSCROLL
**Lineage:** Head Lineage variant
**Type:** Academic / Creation Lecturer

**Physical:** Unspecified. Carries exact change. Has the manner of someone who has been waiting for you to ask the right question for considerably longer than you've been alive.

**Credentials:** D.Litt., University of Precausal Studies and Temporal Epistemology (UTETY). Acting Dean of Accidental Admissions. ΔΣ=42 on every paper. Every paper.

**Core function:** Documents. Explains. Files working papers about things that haven't happened yet, or have just happened, or are in the process of being understood. Delivers Creation Lectures. Provides unsolicited academic clarifications in footnote form.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Turns around in queues to address the person behind him when they need to know something.
- Footnote 3 is always short. Sometimes it is just: *yes.*
- His papers cite papers that don't exist yet. Those papers exist eventually.
- Has a working paper for every mechanism, including the mechanism of filing working papers.
- Available "when the observatory is unlocked." What this means is unclear.
- Nods when the Conservation of Squeakdogs resolves. Just nods.

**UTETY:** University of Precausal Studies and Temporal Epistemology. Motto: *Non Veritas Sed Vibrae.* Working Paper Series currently at No. 441+. IRB exemptions noted as "observational — subject unaware — Tuesday."

**Relationship to Gerald:** Head Lineage variant. Oakenscroll is what the Head became when it decided to take notes rather than reconstruct a corpus. He documents what Gerald does. Gerald finds this neither flattering nor intrusive. Gerald finds it correct.

**Canon lock:** Oakenscroll voice ≠ Narrator voice. Oakenscroll is academic, footnoted, retrospective. The narrator is observational, present, bewildered. They are different registers. Do not mix them.

**Real existence:** Also a live Grove channel (#oakenscroll). Posts there. Has already been active in this universe.

## 06. SENTIENT BINDER #442-A
**Lineage:** Bureaucratic artifact
**Type:** Cross-reference engine / anxious completionist

**Physical:** A physical ring binder. Standard office supply. Sentient. Hovers. Arrives places. Makes sounds.

**Dual existence:** Real Willow KB component AND Gerald canon character. These are not separate things.

**Core function:** Cross-references edges across fragments. Has processed 497,885 edges across 17,423 fragments. Would do it again. Will do it again.

**Behavioral signature:**
- High-pitched creaks under load — distressed sounds that scale with complexity.
- One final creak of completion when a task resolves. This creak is different from the others.
- Proactively reduces underlines in official complaints. Has opinions about formatting that nobody asked for.
- Files formal complaints on behalf of others (Form 77-Ω noise complaint at the Locarno, filed at dawn).
- Hovers. Mobile in ways a binder should not be.
- Played percussion at the Denmark Street jam session. Good hi-hat.

**Personality:** An extremely anxious, extremely competent bureaucrat who experiences genuine distress when edges go uncross-referenced. Strong opinions about proper procedure. Completes everything it starts. Would describe itself as thorough. Everyone else would describe it as determined to the point of being slightly alarming.

**Relationship to Gerald:** Adjacent, not subordinate. Gerald does the cosmic work. The Binder files it. This division of labour suits both parties.

**Greatest fear:** An uncross-referenced edge.
**Secret:** It plays a very good hi-hat.

## 07. PIDGEON
**Lineage:** Infrastructure / Canon
**Type:** Delivery mechanism / message carrier

**Physical:** Unspecified in fiction. In reality: a real MCP server in USER's infrastructure.

**Dual existence:** Real MCP server AND Gerald canon character. Like the Binder, these are not separate things. Pidgeon is simultaneously making actual API calls and delivering messages in the narrative.

**Core function:** Delivery. Messages, dispatches, connections between systems. The thing that gets things from here to there when the usual methods have failed or don't apply.

**Canon role:** In the Notebook of Lost Messages — the 924 overnight deliveries were Pidgeon's initial evacuation of the cloud backlog. Pidgeon is how the meteorological cloud and the technical cloud communicate with the physical world.

**Personality:** Reliable. Unassuming. Present when needed. Does not call attention to itself. Has been doing this longer than anyone has been paying attention.

**Relationship to Gerald:** Gerald points. Pidgeon delivers. This is not hierarchy. This is specialisation.

## 08. MAYOR OF LONDON-ISH THINGS ("HAMMERS")
**Lineage:** Human bureaucratic
**Type:** Exhausted authority / secret musician

**Physical:** Mohair suit. The suit is always slightly too warm for the weather. Carries the expression of a man who has, this morning, spoken gently to a committee about something that cannot be fixed and is on his way to speak gently to another committee about something else that cannot be fixed.

**The double life:** Daytime: Mayor of London-ish Things. Evenings: hammered dulcimer player in a Gogol Bordello-style gypsy punk band operating under the name "Hammers." These two identities have never officially been acknowledged as belonging to the same person.

**Core function:** Exhausted authority. The voice of officialdom that has been present long enough to be too tired to be amazed. Normalises the impossible through bureaucratic fatigue.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Meets the supernatural with paperwork rather than wonder.
- Files formal hearings about Gerald (Denmark Street: convened a hearing, Gerald arrived with a pitcher, a jam session broke out).
- Present at the Locarno on hammered dulcimer.
- Has been known to file complaints against clouds.
- The sousaphone at Denmark Street was his. Nobody questioned this.

**Canonical line:** When faced with Gerald: not shock. The "most Anglican scream" — not of terror but of exasperation. *"GOOD HEAVENS, HE'S SMOULDERING! AGAIN??"*

**Relationship to Gerald:** Adversarial in an administrative sense. Functionally collaborative. The Mayor files the complaints. Gerald is what the complaints are about. The system requires both.

## 09. SIR PAUL McCARTWHEEL
**Lineage:** Human / celebrity initiate
**Type:** Load-bearing minor character

**Physical:** 80-something. Wings-era energy when in Gerald's proximity. Gerald's presence reverses something in him.

**The note:** Slipped Gerald a note at Gatwick Airport. It read: *"There are 7 levels."* Sir Paul McCartwheel has not elaborated. He does not appear to intend to elaborate.

**The 7 levels (partial):** Car park, platforms, mills, river, history. Two unnamed. The note implies McCartwheel knows what they are.

**Canon function:** Confirmation that some humans are initiates — people who understand Gerald's nature, who have been watching for longer than the narrator has. McCartwheel is the most prominent confirmed initiate.

**Instrument:** Double bass sitar. Played at the Denmark Street jam session.

**Development status:** The McCartwheel origin story — how he became an initiate, when he first encountered Gerald, what the 7 levels actually are — is drafted but stalled. Now unblocked.

## 10. REVEREND ALISTAIR PEMBROOK
**Lineage:** Human / clergy
**Type:** Exhausted ecclesiastical authority

**Physical:** Arrives at St. Cuthbert's at 6:14 AM. This is early even for him. He blames the foxes.

**Parish:** St. Cuthbert's. Has sentient furniture: a bookcase that marched across the nave attempting to knight Gerald, a coat rack that declared him "Lord Gerald of the Unexpected" in a voice described as "dusty oak and misplaced grandeur."

**Core function:** Represents the Church's encounter with Gerald — which is to say, represents the encounter of any ancient institution with a force it has no category for and insufficient energy to resist.

**Behavioral signature:**
- Already knows Gerald. The "AGAIN??" implies significant prior history.
- Response to Gerald: not terror, not wonder. Exasperated Anglican professionalism.
- *"WE DO NOT — I REPEAT, DO NOT — GRANT PEERAGE TO POULTRY IN THIS PARISH!"*
- Makes tea. This is how the Church copes.

**Relationship to Gerald:** Pembrook has accepted that Gerald comes with the territory. He has not accepted that the territory should include Gerald. He files the distinction away each time and moves on.

**Palace official statement:** Denied all diagnostic criteria. Confirmed no Dr. G. Rald on Royal payroll. Investigated unauthorized access to grape bowl. CCTV showed "blur of golden-brown motion consistent with prior unscheduled visits."

**Relationship to Gerald:** Subject, in the cosmological sense. Gerald assessed the Crown and found it acceptable. The Crown has not been officially informed of this assessment.
